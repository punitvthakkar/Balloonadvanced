Adaptive Balloon Risk Game

How to Play:

You'll be presented with a series of balloons, one at a time.

For each balloon, you have two choices:

  • Pump: Increases the balloon size and adds points (5 points per pump).
  • Cash Out: Banks your current points and moves to the next balloon.

Be careful! If you pump too much, the balloon will pop and you'll lose points for that balloon.

The Challenge: Different colors have different hidden capacities (risk levels - High, Medium, Low).

  • One color pops easily (High Risk).
  • One color has medium capacity (Medium Risk).
  • One color can be pumped many times (Low Risk).

The color-to-risk mapping is RANDOMIZED each game! Pay attention to how many pumps each color seems to handle to figure out the pattern for *this* game and maximize your score!
